3.RGB格式与HSI格式之间的转换

RGB ==> HSI

rgb2hsi()

    figure('name','3.RGB转HSI格式','NumberTitle','off');
    rgb3=rgb;
    [H,S,I]=rgb2hsi(rgb3);
    subplot(221);imshow(rgb3);title('RGB原图像');
    subplot(222);imshow(H);title('H分量');
    subplot(223);imshow(S);title('S分量');
    subplot(224);imshow(I);title('I分量');
